[Change Enemy State]

 

Function

·Changes the status of an enemy.

 

Settings

 

·Under [Target], choose the enemy character whose status is to be changed from the dropdown menu.

 

·Under [Operand], choose one of the following for changing the status.

oAdd: Places a status effect on the enemy.

oRemove: Removes the enemy's status effect.

 

·Choose the status effect type from the dropdown menu under [State].
