[Change Gold]

 

Function

·Increase or decrease the player's money.

 

Settings

 

·Define the operation under [Operation].

Increase Money

Increases amount of money in possession.

Decrease Money

Decreases amount of money in possession.

 

·Under [Operand], choose the value of money to change the player's money by.

Constant

Increases or decreases the amount by a fixed value. Takes a numerical value between 1~999999 as input.

Variable

Increases or decreases the amount by the value stored in a variable. For example, if variable no. 2 is specified, which contains the value 5 when the command is carried out, the amount of money will change by 5.

The variable to be used can be specified by clicking the [...] button to bring up the [Variables] window.
