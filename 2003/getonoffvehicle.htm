[Get On/Off Vehicle]

 

Function

·Put the player onto a vehicle, or release the player from a vehicle if already on board.

·This makes it possible to enter or leave a vehicle (normally done via keypress) via events.

 

Settings

·There are no settings.

·As with entering/leaving a vehicle under normal conditions, the following conditions must be met when the command is carried out. If the conditions are not fulfilled, this command will have no effect.

 

i.When entering a ship (Boat, Ship)

The ship must be on the tile next to the player, in the direction that the player is facing.

 

ii.When leaving a ship (Boat, Ship)

The player has passage on the tile that the player will appear on (the tile next to the ship, in the direction that the ship is facing).

 

iii.When entering an Airship

The player is on the same tile as the airship.

 

iv.When leaving an Airship

The player has passage on the tile that the player will appear on when leaving the airship.
